Compatible with iOS 8 to 14.

Requires RocketBootstrap greater than v1.0.6 (Add official repo: apt.autotouch.net to install the latest version of RocketBootstrap)

A macro tool for recording and playing back the touch actions.

AutoTouch is used to record and playback your operations in your mobile device, such as iPhone, iPad, iPad Mini, iPod Touch.
